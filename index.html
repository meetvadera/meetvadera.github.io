Meet P. Vadera

I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon, where I work on building large-scale deep learning solutions (including but not limited to large language models) for Alexa Smart Home. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, where I was advised by Benjamin Marlin. My research interests include structured text generation using large language models, Bayesian deep learning and robustness in deep learning. In the past, I have also worked in the areas of mHealth, and medical imaging.

Prior to joining the Ph.D. program, I was working as a Member of Technical Staff at Innovaccer building products on big-data stack to help healthcare organizations deliver better care. I earned my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degree from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Gandhinagar, where I majored in Mechanical Engineering and had a minor in Computer Science and Engineering.
